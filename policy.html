Payment in cash or e-transfer only. NO CARD accepted.

At the moment, appointments are 1:1 only. One additional guest may be allowed with prior permission — please let me know in advance. No minors under 15 years old are permitted. Guests may be asked to leave if they cause a disturbance.

I usually treat appointments as silent sessions so I can focus on your nails and keep us on schedule. We can chat a bit during prep if you’d like, but once I start applying polish, I’ll need to stay quiet to concentrate. You’re welcome to watch a show or movie on my TV, or if you’d prefer music instead, just let me know. And of course, if you have any questions, feel free to ask!

There is a 10-minute grace period, but let me know asap if you’re going to be late. After 10 minutes sharp, a late fee of 10$ or more will be charged, or I may cancel the appointment (cancellation fee will apply).

1. Appointments booked throuigh booking link:

Cancellation

All cancellations result in a 50$ cancellation fee. Cancellations made with less than 72 hours’ notice will incur a cancellation fee of 50$ + 50% of the full service price

Rescheduling

Rescheduling with less than 48 hours’ notice will incur a 50$ fee. To confirm the new appointment, the fee must be paid. If the fee is not paid, the appointment will be treated as a cancellation, and the applicable cancellation fee will be charged.

Notes

E-transfer is recommended for cancellation and rescheduling fee, to avoid additional processing fees from Square. If the fee is not paid via e-transfer within 24 hours, the fee will be charged to the card on file, plus an additional 20% processing fee to cover transaction costs.

2. Appointments booked booked through DM/email

Cancellation

All cancellations result in forfeiture of your deposit. Cancellations made with less than 72 hours’ notice will incur an additional 50% fee based on the full service price. This fee is separate from your deposit.

Rescheduling

Rescheduling with less than 48 hours’ notice will forfeit 50% of your deposit. To confirm the new appointment date, the remaining 50% of the deposit must be paid. If it is not paid, the appointment will be treated as a cancellation, and the cancellation fee above will apply

Failure to pay the cancellation fee, or follow the policy will result in being denied future bookings.

3. Additional Terms

  • Clients may reschedule an appointment up to 3 times.
  • Partial payments will not confirm a rescheduled appointment; full fee must be paid.
  • All times are local to Vancouver.
  • Cancellation/reschedule fees are based on the full service price before any discounts.

When you book a detailed design or a service that requires longer time than my usual time slot, I’ll have to reserve 2 time slots just for you. If you change to a much simpler design less than 1 week before the appointment, an additional charge will apply, as the reserved time likely can’t be rebooked on short notice.
