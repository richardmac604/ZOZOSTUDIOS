Hi there :)

Welcome to my jewel garden. If you're looking for a calm, peaceful, and relaxing environment with unique nail designs, you're in the right place.

People usually call me Zoey, Zo or Z (/zee:/). I'm the owner of ZozoStudio and currently the only designer here.

I’m an introvert and tend to open up slowly, but I’m a good listener, and my regulars often say they feel safe and comfortable confiding in me. I love creating a space where people can just breathe, turn their brain off, and leave with something that lifts them up — many clients say their nails brighten their everyday life

I'm always down to chat a bit during prep, but because my detailed work requires precision and focus, I usually keep appointments mostly quiet once polish or design work begins. You’re welcome to watch a show, movie on my TV or listen to music while I work.

I’m understanding, but I keep clear boundaries and strict policies. If you value respectful, mindful interactions and want a peaceful studio with unique, one-of-a-kind designs, you’ll feel right at home here 🩶
