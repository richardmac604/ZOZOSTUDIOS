I. Services:

1 offer gel manicure & gel extension, 3 types of design services, and gel removal. No pedicure service at the
moment.

Gel Overlay: Gel manicure on natural nails
Gel Extensions: Apres Gel X

Each service has:
-A short overview (must-read for everyone).
-A "Read Before Booking" section - additional info required only for the specific service you choose.

Monthly Design

Choose from any of my original designs that I update monthly. Most designs can be chosen in person on your appointment.

Ensure your nails are clean and free of polish. Bring any specific design preferences or reference photos if available. Monthly designs are updated regularly, so check the collection page for current options.

Design Recreation

For clients who want me to replicate a nail photo that is
not part of my Monthly Design collection.

Provide clear, high-quality photos of the design you want recreated. Note that complex designs may incur additional charges based on time and materials. I can recreate from social media or personal photos.

Freestyle Design

Custom design in my style based on non-nail photos.

Describe your inspiration, theme, or mood for the design. I will create a custom nail art based on your input. This allows for unique, personalized designs not available in standard collections.

Removal

This service safely removes existing gel polish or extensions. Please inform me if you have sensitive skin or allergies. The process takes about 15-20 minutes and includes basic nail care afterward.

II. Policy
Payment is due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and digital payments like Apple Pay. Deposits may be required for complex designs.
Please arrive 5-10 minutes early. No outside food or drinks allowed. Children under 12 must be supervised. Maintain a respectful environment for all clients.
Late arrivals may result in shortened service time or rescheduling. A $10 late fee applies for arrivals more than 15 minutes past appointment time.
Cancellations require 24 hours notice. Same-day cancellations or no-shows may forfeit deposit. Rescheduling is allowed once per appointment with proper notice.
Design changes can be made up to 24 hours before appointment. Changes within 24 hours may incur additional fees depending on complexity.
Book through our website or call directly. First-time clients should book consultation appointments. Deposits required for new clients.
