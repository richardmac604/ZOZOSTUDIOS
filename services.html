Monthly Design

Choose from any of my original designs that I update monthly. Most designs can be chosen in person on your appointment.

There are 4 design tiers: Intermidiate, Complex, Detailed, Intricate. For deailted or intricate tier, it is advised to send the design in advance so i can prep properly - it doesn't have to be sent right when you book, but the sooner ther better.

Check Monthly Design Collection to see the designs.

Freestyle Design

Custom design in my style based on non-nail photos.

Photos could be anything from fashion, photography to cakes, ceramics and other visuals

There are 2 key things to consider:

  • Complexity levels/tiers: Complex or Extra
  • Inspo option: There are 3 ways to choose your freestle inspo (explained below)

upon booking, if no information is provided, (or missing info) regarding this provided, i'll assume that you're letting me decide.

Freestyle Inspo Options:

Option 1:

Ill have full creative control.

Option 2:

choose from my available inspo. See the "Inspo" story highlight. There are mostly non-repeatable, make sure to send your chosen inspo when booking to claim it.

Option 3:

We use your moodboard/inspo board (must be sent at least 3 days prior to the appointment).

If inspo isn't sent in time, the result may not meet expectations. Your inspo board can include nail pics, but only 1-2 out of every 5 images can be nails. The rest should be non-nail photos for it to count as Freestyle. Only inspo that does fit my style will be used (see “Fav FS” or “Monthly” story highlight for examples). I'll do my best to reflect your vision, but the final design may not follow your inspo exactly.

See freestyle design examples on FAQ

Removal

I only accept gel on natural nails or Gel X extensions for removal. Regular polish may be accepted with charge.

I do not accept acrylic, dual form, hard gel extension, etc. Thick nail sets with lots of charms, stones, 3D elements might not be accepted for foreign removal.

Removal time and price depend on the type of nails, thickness, and any charms or 3D elements, etc.
